SO,WHO AM I? HERE'S MY STORY

I'm Diana Julia R. Sudario, born on July 29, 2006, and raised in the beautiful town of Baybay, Carigara, Leyte. I recently graduated from Jugaban National High School as a proud Grade 12 HUMSS student, and I'm now a college student, working even harder every day to reach my dreams. Growing up in a big family with eleven siblings taught me about love, patience, and perseverance. My parents, despite the many challenges, continue to be my greatest inspirations, showing me that hard work and heart can get you through anything.

Outside of my studies, I find joy in simple things watching K-dramas,dancing, listening to music, playing badminton, and spending time with my loved ones. Enhypen's music lifts my spirit, and memories with my best friend, Kathleen, remind me that life is always better when shared with someone who truly understands you. I may be shy, but I believe that with enough passion and courage, even the quietest dreams can roar loud enough to be heard.
